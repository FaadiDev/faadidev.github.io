FAQs

What products do you sell ?

We offer a wide range of gaming products, including video games, gaming consoles, gaming accessories, gaming pc/laptops, and more.

Do you offer cash-on delivery ?

Yes, cash-on delivery is only available in Pakistan at the moment. We're working on expanding this facility throughout worldwide.

Is it safe to make a purchase on your website ?

Yes, our website is secure, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information during the checkout process.

Do you offer international shipping ?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location also if you can cover the shipping expenses, through FedEx/DHL.

What is your return policy ?

Our return policy allows you to return products within a specified period after purchase.

How can I contact customer support ?

For any inquiries or assistance, you can reach our customer support team through the Contact Us page on our website. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

Do you offer warranty ?

Yes, we do offer a 1-year limited warranty (excluding burn/short circuit/physical damage) on all of our new products. We also offer a 1-month replacement warranty for all used parts.

Do you buy used parts ?

Yes, we do buy used parts after thorough inspection and testing.
